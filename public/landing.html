Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?

10-15 minutes for Shopify or WooCommerce using our 1-click integration. For other platforms (Crystal Commerce, Binderpos, custom sites), setup typically takes same day with our support team helping you.

Do you support other TCGs besides Yu-Gi-Oh!?

Currently Yu-Gi-Oh! only. Magic: The Gathering and Pok√©mon TCG support is coming in Q2 2025. Want to be notified? Join our waitlist.

Can I customize the design to match my store?

Yes! Basic color and font customization is included in all plans. Pro tier includes full custom CSS for pixel-perfect branding. Enterprise tier offers white-label option with all DeckMage branding removed.

What happens if cards aren't in stock?

DeckMage automatically shows only in-stock items. If a specific version is out of stock, it will suggest available alternatives (different sets/rarities). You can configure this behavior in your dashboard.

Do customers need to create an account to use the deck builder?

No! DeckMage works just like any other "add to cart" feature. Customers paste their deck, add cards to cart, and checkout normally through your existing system. No extra login required.

Can I prioritize certain card versions to increase margins?

Yes! Pro tier lets you set priority rules (e.g., always select your best-selling versions first, or prioritize high-margin items). You can update these rules anytime in your dashboard.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

No contract required! All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard - no questions asked, no cancellation fees.

What kind of support do you offer?

Basic plan includes email support (24-48hr response). Pro plan includes priority support (12hr response). Enterprise plan includes dedicated account manager and SLA guarantee.
